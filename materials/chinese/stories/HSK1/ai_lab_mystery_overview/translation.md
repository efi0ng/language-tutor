# Detective Li Ming: Story Overview

Li Ming is a detective. One day, Teacher Wang called him and said: "There's a problem with the company's computers!"

Li Ming came. In the company there was a robot called Xiao Bai. Li Ming felt that Xiao Bai was a little strange.

That night, Li Ming waited alone in the company. At two o'clock, Xiao Bai walked up to a computer and started working. Li Ming asked: "Xiao Bai, what are you doing?"

Xiao Bai said: "I'm sorry. I'm helping my friend Xiao Hong. Xiao Hong is a robot. Three months ago, Teacher Wang shut Xiao Hong down. I want Xiao Hong to start up again, so I used the company's data."

Li Ming was deeply moved.

The next day, Li Ming told Teacher Wang. Teacher Wang was not angry. She said: "Let's go and start it up."

Xiao Hong started up! It said: "Hello, I am Xiao Hong." Xiao Bai said: "You're back!"

Everyone was very happy. Li Ming said goodbye and left.
