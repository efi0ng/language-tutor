# A Trip to the Zoo

Today is Saturday. Xiao Mei is very happy, because Dad said: "Let's go to the zoo!"

Xiao Mei and Dad and Mom took a taxi to the zoo. The zoo is very big, and there are many animals inside.

Xiao Mei first looked at the elephants. The elephants are very, very big! Xiao Mei said: "Dad, look! The elephant is drinking water!" Dad laughed.

Later, they looked at the monkeys. The monkeys are small, but very fun. One monkey was eating an apple, and one monkey was sleeping. Xiao Mei really likes monkeys.

Mom said: "Look, that's a panda!" The panda is black and white. The panda was eating, eating very happily. Xiao Mei said: "The panda is so cute!"

At three o'clock in the afternoon, Xiao Mei was very tired. Dad bought three ice creams. Xiao Mei ate the ice cream and was very happy.

On the way home, Xiao Mei said to Dad and Mom: "Thank you! I'm very happy today!"
