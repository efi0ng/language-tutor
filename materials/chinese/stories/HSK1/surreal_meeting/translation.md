# The Cat Comes to the Meeting

Today, my friends and I were together. We sat around a big table. On the table there was water and tea.

A cat arrived. The cat sat on the table. The cat drank my water and also drank my tea.

"Whose cat is this?" I asked.

My friend said, "I don't know."

The cat looked at me and said, "Hello! My name is Xiaobai. I am your teacher!"

We were all very happy. This cat could speak! Today was a very good day.
