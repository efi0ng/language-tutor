# Detective Li Ming: A Good Ending

The next morning, Li Ming told Teacher Wang everything.

Teacher Wang listened, and was not angry. She thought quietly for a moment and said: "Xiao Hong… I thought it couldn't be used anymore, so I shut it down. I didn't know Xiao Bai missed Xiao Hong so much."

Li Ming said: "Xiao Bai was helping its friend. It is a very good robot."

Teacher Wang nodded, and tears appeared in her eyes. She said: "Alright, let's go and start Xiao Hong up."

They came to a small room. Inside, a red robot stood quietly. Teacher Wang walked over and pressed a button.

Everyone waited in silence.

After a moment, Xiao Hong's eyes lit up. It slowly began to move, looked around, and then said: "Hello. I am Xiao Hong."

Xiao Bai walked over quickly and said: "Xiao Hong, you're back!"

Xiao Hong looked at Xiao Bai and said: "Xiao Bai, was it you who helped me? Thank you."

Everyone was very happy.

Teacher Wang said to Li Ming: "Thank you, Mr. Li. You didn't only solve the problem — you also helped Xiao Bai and Xiao Hong."

Li Ming smiled and said: "This is a very good outcome. I'm happy to have been able to help everyone."

He said goodbye to everyone and walked out of Starlight Computer Company.
