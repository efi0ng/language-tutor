# Detective Li Ming: Li Ming Arrives

Li Ming is a detective. One day, he received a phone call. The call was from Teacher Wang.

Teacher Wang worked at Starlight Computer Company. She said: "Mr. Li, something very important has disappeared from our company's computers! Please come and help."

Li Ming said: "Alright, I'll come right away."

Li Ming arrived at Starlight Computer Company. It was a very large company, with many computers inside. Teacher Wang was waiting for him at the entrance. She said: "Thank you for coming. I'm very worried."

Li Ming asked: "What has gone missing?"

Teacher Wang said: "Data from the computers. It was there yesterday, and today it's just gone."

Teacher Wang led Li Ming into the company. Many people were working inside. At that moment, a white robot walked over.

Teacher Wang said: "This is Xiao Bai. It is our company's robot, and it helps us with a lot of work every day."

Xiao Bai said: "Hello, Mr. Li. Welcome to Starlight Computer Company."

Li Ming said: "Hello, Xiao Bai." But inside he felt a little strange. Xiao Bai kept watching him, as if thinking about something.

Li Ming decided to take a careful look at this matter.
