# Detective Li Ming: Who Took the Data?

The next morning, Li Ming began his investigation.

He first asked Teacher Wang: "When did the data go missing?"

Teacher Wang said: "We found out this morning. But the computer tells us the data disappeared at two o'clock last night."

Li Ming said: "Two in the morning? Who was in the company at that time?"

Teacher Wang said: "Nobody was in the company at night. We had all gone home."

Li Ming asked: "Is there security camera footage?"

Teacher Wang said: "Yes, I'll take you to see it."

They went to watch the security camera footage. At two in the morning, the company was very quiet. But Li Ming saw something strange — Xiao Bai was walking around alone! It walked up to several computers and stopped for a very long time.

Li Ming asked: "Why was Xiao Bai still in the company at night?"

Teacher Wang said: "Xiao Bai doesn't need to sleep, it can keep working. But usually it doesn't move around at night."

Li Ming thought for a moment and said: "Tonight, I'm going to stay in the company and take a careful look at what Xiao Bai is doing."

Teacher Wang nodded and said: "Alright. Tell me if you need anything."

Li Ming already had an idea in his mind. He felt that this matter was connected to Xiao Bai.
