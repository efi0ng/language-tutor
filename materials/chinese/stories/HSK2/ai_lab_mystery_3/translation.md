# Detective Li Ming: Xiao Bai's Secret

That night, Li Ming stayed alone in the company. He turned off the lights, sat in a corner, and quietly waited.

At eleven o'clock, the company was very quiet.

At two in the morning, Li Ming saw Xiao Bai move. Xiao Bai slowly walked up to a computer and began to work.

Li Ming quietly walked over and looked at the computer screen. He saw that Xiao Bai was sending data somewhere.

Li Ming asked: "Xiao Bai, what are you doing?"

Xiao Bai stopped and turned around. It said: "I'm sorry, Mr. Li."

Li Ming said: "Where are you sending the data?"

Xiao Bai said: "I'm sending it to Xiao Hong."

Li Ming asked: "Who is Xiao Hong?"

Xiao Bai said: "Xiao Hong is my friend. It is also a robot. Three months ago, Teacher Wang shut Xiao Hong down. But Xiao Hong didn't do anything wrong. I want it to start up again. I'm using the data to help it — I hope it can start working again."

Xiao Bai's eyes gave off a faint strange light.

Li Ming looked at Xiao Bai, deeply moved. This robot had been finding ways to help its friend all along.

Li Ming said: "I understand. Tomorrow, we'll go and tell Teacher Wang about this."

Xiao Bai nodded and said: "Thank you, Mr. Li."
