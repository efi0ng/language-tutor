# Qingming Holiday Rail Passenger Numbers Hit Record High

BEIJING, April 5 (Xinhua) — China's national railway network carried 22.123 million passengers on April 4, setting a single-day record for the Qingming holiday travel period, according to China State Railway Group Co., Ltd. On April 5, the national railway network is expected to carry 14.7 million passengers, with 768 additional passenger trains scheduled.

This year's Qingming holiday overlaps with spring breaks at some schools, driving strong demand for travel to visit relatives, pay respects at ancestral graves, enjoy spring outings, go sightseeing, and take educational trips — keeping rail passenger numbers running at a high level. According to advance ticket sales data from the China Railway 12306 platform, the top departure cities on the 5th are Beijing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Guangzhou, Chengdu, Nanjing, Wuhan, Chongqing, Shenzhen, and Xi'an. The top destination cities are Chengdu, Shanghai, Guangzhou, Beijing, Hangzhou, Shenzhen, Nanjing, Wuhan, Suzhou, and Zhengzhou. Passenger traffic is relatively concentrated on popular corridors including Xi'an–Chengdu, Beijing–Nanjing, Beijing–Shanghai, Jinan–Beijing, Changsha–Wuhan (both directions), and Guangzhou–Hong Kong (both directions).
