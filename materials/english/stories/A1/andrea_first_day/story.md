# Andrea's First Day

Andrea is from Colombia. She is new in the city. She does not know many people. She feels lonely and a little sad.

One day, her neighbor Maria knocks on her door.
"Do you want to come to the gym with me?" Maria asks.
Andrea is nervous. "I don't know," she says. "I never go to the gym."
"It's okay," Maria says. "I can help you."

At the gym, Andrea looks at the machines. They are big and strange. She does not know what to do. She tries to lift a small weight. It is hard. Her arms hurt.

A trainer named Carlos sees her. He smiles. "Don't worry," he says. "Everyone starts from zero. You are doing well."

Andrea feels better. She tries again.

That evening, Andrea is very tired. But she is also happy. She thinks: "Tomorrow, I will go back."

| Word | English |

| nervous | feeling worried or a little afraid |
| neighbor | a person who lives near you |
| gym | a place where people exercise |
| machine | a big tool you use to exercise |
| lift | to pick something up |
| weight | a heavy object used for exercise |
| trainer | a person who teaches you to exercise |
| hurt | to feel pain |
| strange | not familiar; new and different |
