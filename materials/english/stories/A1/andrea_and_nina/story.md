# Andrea and Nina

Andrea's plane lands at Heathrow. She is tired but excited. She has two big suitcases. They are very heavy. She also has a small carrier. Inside, her cat Nina is sleeping.

Andrea goes to the immigration area. There is a long queue. Andrea waits. Nina makes a small sound. "Shh, Nina," Andrea says. "We are almost there."

At the front, the officer is serious. He looks at Andrea's passport.

"Why are you coming to the UK?" he asks.

"I live here now," Andrea says. "My husband is here."

The officer sees the carrier. "Is that a cat?"

"Yes. Her name is Nina. I have all her papers."

The officer takes the papers. He reads them slowly. He looks at his computer. He does not speak.

"Please wait here," he says. He calls a colleague.

Andrea's heart beats fast. She looks at Nina. Nina looks back at her with big eyes.

A woman in a green uniform comes. She is a vet.

She smiles at Andrea. "Hello. I need to check Nina. Please come with me."

Andrea picks up her heavy bag. She follows the vet. She hopes everything is okay.
