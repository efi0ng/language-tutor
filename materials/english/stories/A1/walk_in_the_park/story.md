# A Walk in the Park

Tom and Lisa are happy today. The sun is warm and the sky is blue. They go to the park.

"Look at the flowers!" Lisa says. "They are so beautiful!" Tom smiles. "Yes, I like the red ones."

They walk slowly near the lake. There are ducks on the water. Lisa gives bread to the ducks. Tom laughs. "They are very hungry!"

They sit on a bench under a big tree. Tom has two cups of coffee. They drink and talk.

"I love this park," Lisa says.

"Me too," Tom says. "Let's come again tomorrow."
