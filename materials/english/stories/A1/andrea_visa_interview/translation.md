# The Important Interview — Story Summary

Andrea is nervous. She has a visa interview next week. She wants to live in the UK with her husband, Tom. Tom is English. He lives in London.

Andrea visits her friend Sofia. Sofia passed her visa interview last year. She knows what questions they ask.

Sofia asks Andrea about Tom's birthday, his address, and his job. Andrea knows his birthday and address, but she does not know the name of the hospital where he works. She writes it in her notebook.

Sofia asks about Tom's parents' names. Andrea knows them: his mother is Susan, his father is David. She also knows how they met (online) and how they talk every day (video calls and messages).

Sofia asks about Tom's hobbies. Andrea knows he loves football and cooking Italian food.

Finally, Sofia asks about Tom's salary. Andrea does not know. She calls Tom and writes the answer in her notebook.

At the end, Andrea reads all her notes: birthday, address, job, salary, parents' names, how they met, how they communicate, hobbies. She closes her notebook and says, "I am ready."
